How Trumould partnered with Mpoint to manufacture the PASSalert — an IoT-based fire detection & alert system enclosure — from first prototype to full-scale delivery.

Client Mpoint
Product PASSalert Enclosure
Industry IoT / Fire Safety
Processes Injection Moulding, 3D Printing, Pad Printing
Project Overview
Building a safer world,
one enclosure at a time

Mpoint needed a rugged, precision-engineered enclosure for their PASSalert IoT fire detection system — a device designed to save lives. Trumould delivered end-to-end, from rapid prototyping to scalable mass production.

The Challenge

Design and manufacture a compact enclosure housing sensitive IoT electronics, a speaker grille for alarm output, and branding elements — all while meeting fire safety industry standards for durability and heat resistance.

The Solution

Trumould's full-service approach — 3D printed prototypes for rapid iteration, precision injection moulds for production, and pad printing for brand identity — delivered a market-ready product in record time.

The Process
A journey of precision

Every great product starts with a vision. Here's how we brought the PASSalert enclosure from concept to production.

1

Mould & Tooling Development

Tool Design DFM Analysis Steel Tooling

Our engineering team conducted a comprehensive Design for Manufacturability (DFM) analysis, optimizing wall thickness, draft angles, and gate locations. Custom steel tooling was precision-machined to produce the PASSalert's distinctive form — including the intricate speaker grille pattern and snap-fit features.

2

Prototyping & Iteration

3D Printing Fit Testing Design Refinement

Using 3D printing technology, we produced rapid prototypes for Mpoint to test electronics fitment, speaker acoustics, and mounting clip functionality. Each iteration refined the enclosure until it met every functional and aesthetic requirement before committing to steel tooling.

3

Mass Production & Delivery

Injection Moulding Pad Printing Quality Control

With validated tooling, we scaled to full production using injection moulding for consistent, high-quality enclosures. Pad printing applied the PASSalert branding and fire safety iconography with precision. Each batch underwent rigorous quality checks before careful packaging and delivery.
